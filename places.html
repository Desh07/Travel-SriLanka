Top Places to Visit in Sri Lanka

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Ancient rock fortress with stunning frescoes and panoramic views. A UNESCO World Heritage site dating back to the 5th century.

Best Time to Visit: January to April

Yala National Park

Home to leopards, elephants, and crocodiles. One of the best places in the world for leopard sightings.

Best Time to Visit: February to July

Galle Fort

A 16th-century Portuguese-built fort with charming colonial architecture, boutique shops, and ocean views.

Best Time to Visit: November to April

Kandy

Cultural capital with the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, beautiful lakes, and traditional dance performances.

Best Time to Visit: January to April, July to August
