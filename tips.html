Sri Lanka Travel Tips

Do's in Sri Lanka

  • Dress modestly when visiting temples (shoulders and knees covered)
  • Remove shoes before entering temples and homes
  • Bargain politely in markets and with tuk-tuk drivers
  • Try local cuisine like hoppers, kottu, and string hoppers
  • Carry small change as many places don't accept large bills
  • Drink bottled or filtered water

Don'ts in Sri Lanka

  • Don't disrespect Buddhist statues or images (never pose with your back to them)
  • Don't display public affection - it's frowned upon
  • Don't use your left hand for eating or giving/receiving items
  • Don't take photos of military installations
  • Don't buy or export ancient artifacts
  • Don't feed or disturb wildlife in national parks

Essential Tips

  • Best time to visit: West/South coasts - December to April; East coast - May to September
  • Get an e-visa before arrival (www.eta.gov.lk)
  • Use the train for scenic journeys (especially Kandy to Ella)
  • Carry sunscreen, hat, and mosquito repellent
  • Local SIM cards are cheap and widely available
  • Standard voltage is 230V with Type D and G plugs
